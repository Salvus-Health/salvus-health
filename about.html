Welcome to The Code Company.
Let us give you the jist.

The Code Company is a new design and development shop based out of Chapel Hill, North Carolina. Despite being a new company we've already earned the business of several companies across the United States and our work is currently being used by 14 million people each month!

Our companny's mission is to provide an excellent product while having a really friendly relationship with our clients. We specialize in building websites for small businesses but are very comfortable working with any sized company.

We really pride ourselves on our stunning eye for design, however, we have a fantastic group of developers who are capable of building anything. Drop us a message, we'd love to get a conversation going.

We strive on providing the best product while also maintaining a fantastic customer service. In fact, you work directly with one of our product managers who keeps you updated on progress.
